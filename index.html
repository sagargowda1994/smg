About Us

Sri MG Electrical Engineers (SMGEE) is a leading Electrical & MEP contracting company in India, founded in 2015.


SMGEE is a leading Mechanical, Electrical (MEP) contracts company in India.
SMGEE was founded in 2015, started in electrical project M/S TLKI, Bidadi, Karnataka.
SMGEE has 2 regional offices nationwide.
SMGEE’s professional team consists of 6 engineers in Electrical, Mechanical, Design, and 18 other well-experienced supervisors, technicians, and draftsmen to support, along with 115+ skilled & unskilled workers.
The company’s knowledge & professional ability is unique and allows multidisciplinary services under the same roof.
The company has long experience in project management, design, and control of projects in various kinds of areas like Industrial, Residential, Commercial, IT & Pharmacy segments.


  • Nationwide operations
  • Experienced engineers & workforce
  • Strong project execution
